Nice to meet you.

CJB Pottery is comprised of three friends - Cha Kyung Lan, Suh Jeong Ah, and Kim Bora - who met and bonded through their love of pottery.
This has been a 5 year experiment in the making of love and curiosity - trying their hands at different kinds of pieces and materials.
CJB Pottery looks forward to continuing this journey of learning and growth as artists.
