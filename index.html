Cha Kyung Lan, Suh Jeong Ah, and Kim Bora -

This has been a 5 year experiment in the making of curiosity and care - trying their hands at different kinds of pieces and materials throughout the process.

CJB Pottery looks forward to continuing this journey of learning and growth as artists.
